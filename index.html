Hoston

About us

Hoston is a small village in the centre of Norway. There is a big variety of nature, hiking options, communities and activities. There is multiple waters within walking distance, great mountains and quiet sanctuary.

Stay

You got multiple options to stay in Hoston if your think outside the mainstream hospitality. With no camping cabins or hotels and the majority of population being farmers you can easily get a room, small cabin or stay in a tent and use the facilities at the farms. Usually they got extra showers and toilets in their cowshed and you can get access if you check with the people living at the farm.
